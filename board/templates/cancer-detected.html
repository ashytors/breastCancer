Breast Cancer

Histopathology images are a crucial tool in breast cancer detection, providing detailed insights into cellular structures and enabling pathologists to diagnose and assess the severity of the disease. Advanced methods such as deep learning have revolutionized breast cancer detection by analyzing histopathology images, offering automated and precise identification of malignant patterns. The use of digital histopathology images in breast cancer research has facilitated the development of robust diagnostic systems, enhancing early detection and improving treatment outcomes.
